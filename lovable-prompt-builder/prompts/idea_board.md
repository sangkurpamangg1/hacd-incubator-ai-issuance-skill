# HACD Idea Board — Cohort 2 (20 Concepts)

Twenty Stack Asset concepts worth forming, organized by category. Every one
passes the same filter: *could this exist on any chain, or does it genuinely
need a PoW-mined, costly-to-fake HACD underneath it?*

How to read the tags:

- **Fit** — how defensible the idea is on PoW-fit + utility clarity:
  Exceptional > Strong > Good > Niche. (See `pow_fit_check.md`.)
- **Build** — rough read on how fast you can get a *working* Lovable demo in
  front of reviewers: Fast > Moderate > Ambitious.
- **★ Stack fit** — ideas that line up with ventures a builder may already run —
  the fastest route to a demo that actually runs, not just reads well.

When a builder gives only a ticker, resolve it here, then confirm category and
why-HACD before generating the Lovable prompt.

## AI Agent

### AGT — AgentPass `Fit: Exceptional · Build: Fast · ★ Ginie / multi-agent stack`
A permanent, tamper-proof identity and reputation container for autonomous AI
agents.
**Why HACD:** agent reputation can't be a database row that resets — HACD makes
it costly to fake and impossible to quietly delete.

## Identity & RWA

### SNTL — Sentinel `Fit: Exceptional · Build: Moderate · ★ HastiIQ / SecureRob`
On-chain identity and service history for physical security devices — cameras,
sensors, patrol robots.
**Why HACD:** a device's formation record needs to be as tamper-evident as the
hardware it's protecting.

### ATST — AttestStack `Fit: Good · Build: Moderate · ★ TokenProof`
Timestamped, immutable containers for compliance attestations and institutional
confirmation letters.
**Why HACD:** regulators want a non-repudiable timestamp, not a spreadsheet
entry — PoW formation gives them exactly that.

### CARB — CarbonMark `Fit: Good · Build: Moderate · ★ JOYO`
A formation record for verified carbon-credit retirements — one HACD per retired
credit batch.
**Why HACD:** carbon credits get double-counted constantly; a costly-to-forge
container makes a retirement final and visible.

### RUTE — RouteToken `Fit: Good · Build: Ambitious`
One HACD per shipment leg — a chain of formation records tracing a physical
good's custody.
**Why HACD:** supply-chain fraud thrives on documents that are cheap to forge;
formation cost fixes that at the source.

### ESTL — EstateLink `Fit: Niche · Build: Moderate`
A symbolic on-chain reference record for shared physical assets — a formation
log, not a legal title.
**Why HACD:** shared-ownership records get lost in group chats; this gives them a
permanent, neutral home.

## Utility

### FLOW — FlowMark `Fit: Good · Build: Fast · ★ GrowStreams`
Formation receipts for active money-streaming commitments — proof a payment
stream actually started.
**Why HACD:** streaming claims are easy to fake on a dashboard; a HACD-backed
formation receipt isn't.

### PRSS — ProofPress `Fit: Strong · Build: Fast`
Immutable authorship timestamps for articles, research notes, or citizen
journalism.
**Why HACD:** authorship disputes need an unfakeable "published first" — HACD's
formation time is exactly that proof.

### CRED — Credentia `Fit: Good · Build: Fast`
Course and certification completions formed as durable, unforgeable HACD-backed
records.
**Why HACD:** diplomas get faked all the time; a record that had to be mined and
Stacked can't be.

### STDY — StudyStack `Fit: Good · Build: Fast`
Dataset and research-citation receipts, so reused data can always be traced back
to its source.
**Why HACD:** research integrity needs a record nobody can quietly edit after the
fact.

## Art

### ECHO — EchoForge `Fit: Good · Build: Moderate`
Generative soundscapes where each HACD's six letters seed a unique short audio
piece via HIP-15.
**Why HACD:** no two HACD share a name, so no two EchoForge pieces can ever share
a seed.

### PIXD — PixelDiamond `Fit: Strong · Build: Ambitious`
An Immutable Stack generative art collection where each piece's palette derives
from its HACD's letters.
**Why HACD:** Immutable Stack exists for exactly this — art that can never be
altered once it's formed.

### RLIC — RelicKeep `Fit: Good · Build: Moderate`
Collectible "relics" whose rarity tier is set by how long their underlying HACD
took to mine.
**Why HACD:** mining difficulty becomes lore — a relic's formation story doubles
as its provenance.

### MUSE — MuseRelay `Fit: Strong · Build: Moderate`
Serialized collaborative fiction where each chapter is formed on its own HACD
lot.
**Why HACD:** a story's canon shouldn't be editable after publication —
formation locks each chapter in place.

## Game & Community

### MAPR — Cartograph `Fit: Strong · Build: Moderate`
A claimable map where players Stack HACD to settle regions of a shared
name-space world.
**Why HACD:** the 16-letter alphabet is itself a finite map — Cartograph turns
naming-space scarcity into the actual game board.

### GULD — GuildForge `Fit: Good · Build: Moderate`
Gaming guild membership and shared inventory anchored to one HACD per guild.
**Why HACD:** a guild's identity should survive a server wipe — HACD gives it a
home outside the game's own database.

### VKEY — VaultKey `Fit: Niche · Build: Fast`
Tiered treasury access — holding a specific HACD unlocks a DAO spending tier.
**Why HACD:** a spending key that can be lost or stolen like a private key, but
never silently duplicated.

### EVNT — EventStamp `Fit: Good · Build: Fast`
Proof-of-attendance for conferences and meetups, formed live at check-in.
**Why HACD:** free-to-mint attendance badges get spammed; a HACD-backed stamp has
real formation cost behind it.

### VOTE — EchoVote `Fit: Niche · Build: Fast`
Lightweight, formed voting receipts for small community decisions and polls.
**Why HACD:** a vote you can verify was actually cast, not just a number that went
up on a website.

### PULS — PulseGuild `Fit: Good · Build: Fast`
Formed streak records for fitness or habit challenges — one HACD per completed
streak.
**Why HACD:** habit trackers reset quietly; a formed streak record is something
you actually earned and can't fudge.

## Quick picks by constraint

- **Fastest credible demo (Fast build + defensible fit):** PRSS (ProofPress),
  AGT (AgentPass), EVNT (EventStamp), CRED (Credentia).
- **Strongest PoW-fit story:** AGT (AgentPass), SNTL (Sentinel), MAPR
  (Cartograph), PIXD (PixelDiamond), MUSE (MuseRelay).
- **Best if you already run a venture (★):** AGT, SNTL, FLOW, ATST, CARB.
- **Most visual / screenshot-friendly for reviewers:** PIXD, ECHO, MAPR, RLIC.

## HACD vocabulary reminder

Each idea forms a **HACD Stack Asset**. In demo copy: HACD is the PoW container,
HAC pays stack cost, Stack is the formation action, and the artifact a reviewer
sees is the **formation record / receipt**. Never frame HACD as "just an NFT" or
claim stack cost guarantees a price.
